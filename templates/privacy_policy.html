Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Introduction

HopFog ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web platform.

Information We Collect
  • Account Information: Username, email address, and password when you register.
  • Usage Data: Messages sent and received through the HopFog network.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and connection data.
  • Location Data: General network location via connected Fog Nodes.
How We Use Your Information
  • To provide and maintain the HopFog service
  • To manage your account and authenticate your identity
  • To monitor network connectivity and deliver messages
  • To send system alerts and SOS notifications
  • To improve our platform and user experience
Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All passwords are hashed and stored securely. Communication over the HopFog network is managed through our ESP32 Fog Node infrastructure.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Data is only accessible to authorized HopFog administrators.

Your Rights
  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Contact your administrator to deactivate your account
Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact your HopFog administrator.
